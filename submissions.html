‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the review process take?

We review all submissions within 2-4 weeks. Due to volume, we may not be able to respond to every submission individually, but we carefully consider each one.

What if my film doesn't fit perfectly?

We appreciate nuanced portrayals. Even if a film has some problematic elements, if it offers valuable positive representation in meaningful ways, we want to hear about it.

Can I suggest multiple films?

Absolutely! Feel free to submit multiple suggestions. We're building a comprehensive resource and value your expertise.

Do you accept animated films?

Yes! As evidenced by our inclusion of Spirited Away and Princess Mononoke, animation can powerfully convey positive masculinity.

How do you handle disagreements?

We welcome constructive critique and diverse perspectives. If you disagree with our analysis of a film, we'd love to hear your viewpoint.
