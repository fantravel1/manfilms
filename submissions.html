Join Our Mission

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after I submit?

We review all submissions carefully. While we can't respond to every submission individually, we deeply appreciate your contribution to this important conversation. Selected submissions may be featured in future blog posts or added to our curated lists.

Can I submit multiple films?

Absolutely! Feel free to submit as many suggestions as you'd like. Each film should be submitted separately so we can give each one proper consideration.

Do you only accept English-language films?

Not at all! We celebrate cinema from around the world. Films in any language are welcome, as healthy masculinity is a universal theme that transcends cultural and linguistic boundaries.

What if my film is still in production?

We're happy to hear about works in progress. Please indicate in your submission that the film is still in production and provide an estimated completion date if possible.
